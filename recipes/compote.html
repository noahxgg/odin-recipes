Warm Berry Compote

Description

This berry compote is made in a slow cooker with frozen berries and orange juice. This is a perfect dessert for the summer! It is perfect by itself or over your favorite icecream

Ingredients

  • 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest together in a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on high until bubbling, about 1 1/2 hours
  3. Stir cornstarch and water together in a cup until fully dissolved. Pour into berry mixture and stir
  4. Cover again and cook until thickened, 5-10 minutes
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
