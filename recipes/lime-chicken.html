Slow Cooker Lime Cilantro Chicken

Recipe Description

This slow cooker cilantro-lime chicken is bursting with flavors and quick to put together! This recipe is perfect for the busy person and makes for a great dinner. This cilantro-lime chicken is perfect to eat as is, or for a fun taco night!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package dry taco seasoning mix
  • 1 medium lime, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 3 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Stir salsa, taco seasoning, lime juice and cilantro together in a slow cooker until well combined.
  3. Add chicken and spoon salsa mixture over top to coat
  4. Cover and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear. Put on low for 6 to 8 hours or high for 4 hours.
  5. Shred chicken in the crock pot with two forks, then mix with the sauce
  6. Enjoy!
