Banana Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavor. Why compromise the banana flavor? friends and family love my recipe and say it's by far the best! It tastes wonderful toasted. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixed in seperate large bowl.
  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended
  5. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until combined
  6. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 60 minutes
  8. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then onto wire rack to cool completely
  9. Slice up and enjoy!
